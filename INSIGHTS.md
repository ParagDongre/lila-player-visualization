# Gameplay Insights

## Insight 1: Kill hotspots near central map areas

Observation:
The heatmap shows a strong concentration of player activity and combat events in the center of the map.

Evidence:
High density of player movement and events in the central coordinates.

Actionable impact:
Level designers may consider redistributing loot or objectives to encourage players to explore other regions.

Metric affected:
Player engagement distribution across map areas.

---

## Insight 2: Certain map regions receive very little player traffic

Observation:
Large sections of the map show very low movement density.

Evidence:
Heatmap shows minimal player positions recorded in those zones.

Actionable impact:
Add loot, missions, or extraction points to increase exploration.

Metric affected:
Map utilization rate.

---

## Insight 3: Player movement paths reveal predictable travel routes

Observation:
Player journey lines show common routes taken by many players.

Evidence:
Multiple overlapping paths along similar coordinates.

Actionable impact:
Level designers could add obstacles, alternate paths, or ambush points.

Metric affected:
Combat frequency and map balance.
